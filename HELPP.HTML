Frequently Asked Questions

What is ACVDMS and who can use it?

The Ayurveda Cohort & Village Data Management System (ACVDMS) is a platform for collecting, managing, and analyzing rural healthcare data. It is used by students, faculty, and administrators to streamline data workflows and track community health activities.

How do I upload data to the system?

Go to the Upload section ‚Üí Click Choose File ‚Üí Select your CSV file ‚Üí Click Validate Data ‚Üí Fix any issues ‚Üí Click Upload Data. Once done, a success message confirms your upload.

How can I generate reports or visualizations?

Navigate to the Reports section, apply filters (village, disease, or date range), and click Generate Report. The system will display charts and summaries you can download as PDF or CSV.

Can I send messages to multiple villages?

Yes. Open the Bulk Messaging module ‚Üí Compose Message ‚Üí Select Villages ‚Üí Write message ‚Üí Send or Schedule. You can attach flyers (under 1 MB) and monitor delivery from the Delivery Log.

What should I do if my upload fails?

Check your internet connection, ensure your file matches the CSV template, and retry after refreshing. Persistent issues should be reported to your admin with a screenshot of the error.
